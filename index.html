How it Works

CONTACT US

If you are facing an unfair medical or pharmacy bill, contact us, and we will try to match you with someone on our team who can help you fight for a fair price.

GET MATCHED

If we can help, we'll reach out to you to learn more about your situation. If we're able, we'll do everything we can to fight for a fair price for you.

FIGHT FOR FAIR

Once you're matched and we have all the information we need, we'll work with you to negotiate with your insurance or providers for a price that's fair.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can you help me reduce my medical bills?

Our medical billing specialists will review your bill to identify irregularities or opportunities for negotiation. Each item typical has a billing code associated with it, and our team will access a vast data network to identify the average price for each code to see where there may have been a mistake or improper billing practice employed.

How can you help with my pharmacy bills?

Our medication billing specialists will review your current medications and costs to identify if you could benefit from switching to a therapeutic equivalent or generic medication that costs less. They will also scour a vast network of data to identify if there is a cheaper pharmacy or method of payment that will reduce your out of pocket costs.

How can I support Fight for Fair?

If you are able, please considering donating to help us in the fight for fair healthcare prices. If you have experience with medical or pharmacy billing, considering joining our team!

Are my contributions tax deductible?

We are working on receiving 501(c)(3) distinction from the IRS which, once granted, will make donations tax deductible.

How much does it cost?

Our goal is to help patients save money, not spend more! We rely entirely on the financial support of our sponsors and individual donors to fund our efforts which allows us to offer our services at no cost.

How long does the process take?

Each case is different, so we don't give a time estimation of how long it might take. But you can rest assured that we will do everything in our power to identify opportunities to reduce your medical or pharmacy bills to a price that's fair.

Can you guarantee that my bills will be lower?

Unfortunately, we cannot make any guarantees that we can lower your bills. Our success will depend on a number of different factors and negotiations.
